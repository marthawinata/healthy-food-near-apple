## General Tips for Your Goal

- **Protein target:** Aim for 1.6–2.2g protein per kg of body weight daily for muscle gain
- **Always choose grilled/steamed over fried** — saves 10–20g fat per meal
- **Skip creamy sauces and dressings** — ask for sauce on the side
- **Add extra protein** — most places charge $2–4 for extra chicken or tofu
- **Avoid coconut-based curries** — coconut milk adds 15–25g fat per serve
- **Double up on sashimi** — purest form of lean protein available in food courts
