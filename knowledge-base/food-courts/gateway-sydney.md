### Quick Tips for Gateway Sydney
- Hero Sushi sashimi is pure protein — double up for a full dinner
- At Billu's, order tandoori/tikka only — avoid butter chicken, korma, and creamy curries
- At Bekya, ask for garlic sauce (toum) on the side — it's oil-based
- At Din Tai Fung, choose steamed dumplings over pan-fried or xiao long bao
- Mad Mex has a nutrition calculator online — customise before you go
- Roll'd rice paper rolls are very low fat but low protein — pair with a protein side
