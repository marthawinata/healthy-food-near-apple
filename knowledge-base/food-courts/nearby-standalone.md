**Best pick here:** Nando's Butterfly Chicken — 59g protein, only 11g fat. Highest protein count on the entire list.
**Lowest calorie:** Subway Chicken Protein Bowl — only 200 kcal with 27g protein.

### Quick Tips
- At Nando's, choose Lemon & Herb for the lowest fat (11g). Extra Hot adds ~6g more fat. Pair with corn on the cob, not chips
- Subway protein bowls remove the bread entirely — skip cheese and mayo, use mustard or vinegar dressing
- At Pokéd, build your own bowl: choose tuna (leanest protein), brown rice base, edamame topping
- Use ponzu or soy-based dressing — avoid creamy mayo sauces
- Skip fried toppings (tempura crunch, wonton chips)
