### Quick Tips for The Galeries
- Sushi Hub & Sushi Hotaru are next to each other — mix sashimi plates for variety
- At Grill'd, always choose panini bun or lettuce wrap over traditional bun
- At Yayoi, stick to grilled (yaki) dishes — avoid tonkatsu and tempura
- At Chat Thai, avoid coconut curries and fried rice — stick to grilled + steamed rice
- At Lotus (Level 1, sit-down), steamed fish is the leanest option on the menu
