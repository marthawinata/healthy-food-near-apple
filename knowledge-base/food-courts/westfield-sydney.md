### Quick Tips for Westfield Sydney
- At GYG, order the burrito bowl with no rice and no cheese for the leanest option (only 4g fat)
- Mero Mero lets you build your own salad — load up on grilled chicken and chickpeas, skip creamy dressings
- At GYG, skip sour cream, cheese, and guac to minimise fat
- Soul Origin salads are pre-made and portion-controlled — good for consistent tracking
- Chat Thai is also at The Galeries — same menu tips apply (grilled, not fried)
