# Health Ranking — All Menu Items

All 25 menu items ranked by **Healthy Score** (protein-to-fat ratio), which measures how much muscle-building protein you get per gram of fat. Higher is better for the goal of increasing muscle while reducing body fat.

**How to read:** A P:F of 5.7:1 means you get 5.7g of protein for every 1g of fat.
